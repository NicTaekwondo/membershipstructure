How the license split works: Under-6 members are always B-License (free). The A-License % applies to all 6+ members only. A-License holders are distributed proportionally across the 6–12 and 13+ age groups by their share of the 6+ pool. B-License 6–12 and 13+ are the remainder within each age group. Promotion revenue = STKD share only; remainder after Moo Keup / 8th–7th / 6th–5th goes to 4th–1st Kup. Large clubs = 100% − small% − medium%.
